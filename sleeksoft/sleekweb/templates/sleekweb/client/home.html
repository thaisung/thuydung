Build a Safe Home for 45 Abandoned Children

Munib Ali is organizing this fundraiser.
Donation protected
Hi, I am Munib and I am helping to build a safe home for these helpless little children in Uganda. Currently, a young local man (with a heart of gold) is using his own limited means to support these 45 children whose parents have either passed away, or they were abandoned on the streets.



You can see the details of how these children are living and the way in which we are supporting them using the links here:

https://linktr.ee/NuriyahChildrensHome

These young children, these innocent souls, need help in every way. They are dependent on the young local man for food and for paying the rent for this temporary shelter. They have no electricity, only one of them is able to go to school based on the school fees the gentleman can afford. They are living off minimal resources.

Our children are our future. I want to help build the future of these young, innocent children, and for that I need your help, please. There are several things that they are in desperate need of, for example:
  • food supply (they cannot be reliant on the one generous soul)
  • their own space. Land can be bought nearby on which they can have their own, secure, peaceful home. What a wonderful thing that would be for them - something we take for granted
  • rent for the current place in which they are sheltering
  • medical costs
  • electricity
  • school fees

Please give as much as you are able to. I am constantly in touch with the local gentleman who is supporting these children I am posting updates and evidence of how the funds collated are being used.

Peace be upon all of you wonderful souls.
